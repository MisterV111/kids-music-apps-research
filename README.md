# Kids Music Apps — Competitive Research

A screenshot-heavy competitive landscape deck covering 8 kids music learning / music play apps targeting ages 6–10. Prepared for Chris Thür (Offbeat) as competitive context for Guitar Animals (Wild Chords rebuild).

**Live deck:** [kids-music-apps-research](https://misterv111.github.io/kids-music-apps-research/)

## Apps covered

1. Piano Maestro (JoyTunes / Simply)
2. Simply Piano — Kids mode (JoyTunes / Simply)
3. Rhythm Cat / Piano Cat (LMuse)
4. Duolingo Music
5. Melody Jams + Loopimal (Yatatoy)
6. Toca Band (Toca Boca / Spin Master)
7. Sago Mini Music Box (Sago Sago / Spin Master)
8. Chrome Music Lab (Google Creative Lab)

Plus appendix: failed attempts in the kids guitar space (Wild Chords, GuitarBots, Rocksmith, Fret Zealot).
